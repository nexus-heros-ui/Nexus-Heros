📖 Nexus Heroes Game Manual

⚡ Quick Start

Goal:
Create heroes, join a world, travel across the map, complete missions, fight villains, earn rewards, and grow stronger.
Fast Setup:

1. Players create heroes in Player Portal.
2. GM opens GM Portal.
3. GM creates a World Code.
4. Players join using the code.
5. GM adds heroes to the world, map, and Player Manager.
6. GM generates a mission.
7. Everyone takes turns.

🦸 Player Handbook

Hero Creation Order:

1. Name hero.
2. Spin Species, Origin, Alignment.
3. Spin Power Chance.
4. If powers are granted, spin Power Count.
5. Spin Power until all slots are filled.
6. Spin all stats.
7. Spin Equipment and Weaknesses.
8. Press Generate Hero.
9. Press Save Hero.

🎲 GM Handbook

GM Controls:

Create world codes, manage teams, move players, control health, generate enemies, create missions, run turns, and decide final rulings.
Turn Order:

1. World events happen.
2. GM controls enemies.
3. Each player acts once.
4. Enemies act.
5. GM presses Next Turn.

⚔ Combat Guide

Each turn a character may Attack, Defend, Move, Use Power, Use Equipment, Help Ally, or Interact.
The GM compares the attacker’s best stat against the defender’s best defensive stat. Damage can be light, serious, critical, or battle-ending.

🌍 World & Travel

To move:
Click a location dot, then press Travel Here. A random travel event may happen.
Travel Events:
Safe Travel, Random Event, Villain Encounter, Merchant, Hidden Treasure, or World Boss.

📜 Missions

Missions include a location, threat, objective, villain, reward, and failure consequence.

👹 Villains & Minions

Enemy Types:

Minion = weak enemy.
Elite = stronger enemy.
Villain = main enemy.
World Boss = huge team threat.
The GM controls enemies manually, but can press Roll AI Action for a fair suggested enemy move.

✨ Powers

Rarity:
Common 50%, Uncommon 30%, Rare 14%, Epic 4.5%, Legendary 1.3%, Nexus 0.2%.

🎒 Equipment

Equipment is gained only if Equipment Chance grants it. Then spin Equipment Count and Equipment.

❓ Help / FAQ

How do I add players?
Generate a hero, then GM adds that hero to World, Map, and Player Manager.
How do enemies work?
GM adds Minions, Elites, or Villains in the Enemy Manager. GM can control them manually or roll AI actions.
How do turns work?
Each player acts once, enemies act, then GM presses Next Turn.
