Nexus Heroes Rulebook

Original Rules

Goal: Create a hero, join or build teams, complete missions, fight villains, earn rewards, and grow stronger over time.
Hero Creation:
1. Choose or generate a name.
2. Spin Power Chance.
3. If powers are granted, spin Power Count.
4. Spin Power Wheel until all power slots are filled.
5. Spin stats, species, origin, alignment, equipment, and weaknesses.
6. Press Generate Entire Hero.
Stats:
Strength, Speed, Durability, Intelligence, Combat, Stamina, and Power Control are ranked from 1 to 14. Higher numbers mean stronger ability.
Power Score:
Power Score is all 7 stat numbers added together. This decides Rank and helps with battles.
Ranks:
Street Hero → Enhanced Hero → Super Hero → Champion → Omega Hero → Cosmic Hero → Nexus Ascendant
Teams:
Players may add heroes to a team. Teams can be used for missions, PvP, and campaign battles. Max team size is 5.
Trading:
No money marketplace. Players may trade item-for-item or gift items if the GM allows it.
GM Role:
The GM creates villains, missions, rewards, battles, and story events.

Power Rarity

Common: 50%
Uncommon: 30%
Rare: 14%
Epic: 4.5%
Legendary: 1.3%
Nexus / Extreme: 0.2%

Power Rules

A power gives the hero a special ability, but it does not automatically win every situation. Power Control, Combat, Intelligence, and GM judgment still matter.

Equipment Rules

A hero must win the Equipment Chance Wheel before gaining equipment. If equipment is granted, spin Equipment Count, then spin Equipment once for each item.

Powers Reference

Enhanced Strength: Increased lifting, striking, and grappling power.
Enhanced Speed: Faster movement and reaction time.
Flight: Allows air travel and aerial combat.
Regeneration: Heals damage over time.
Invulnerability: Greatly reduces physical damage.
Force Fields: Creates protective energy barriers.
Fire Manipulation: Create and control fire.
Ice Manipulation: Create ice, freeze targets, and make barriers.
Energy Projection: Fire blasts, beams, or bursts of energy.
Shapeshifting: Change body shape or appearance.
Super Strength: Major strength beyond enhanced human levels.
Super Speed: Superhuman movement and attack speed.
Telepathy: Read, sense, or send thoughts.
Telekinesis: Move objects with the mind.
Technopathy: Control or communicate with technology.
Teleportation: Instantly move to another place.
Gravity Manipulation: Increase, decrease, or redirect gravity.
Weather Control: Control storms, wind, rain, or lightning.
Mind Control: Influence or control another mind with GM limits.
Energy Absorption: Absorb energy and convert it into power.
Time Manipulation: Slow, speed up, or affect time in limited ways.
Dimensional Travel: Travel between dimensions.
Cosmic Energy: Control powerful cosmic force.
Reality Warping: Change reality in GM-approved ways.
Immortality: Cannot die from normal aging or damage.
Absolute Regeneration: Heal from almost any injury.
Existence Erasure: Extremely rare ability to erase a target from existence.
Nexus Authority: Highest-level Nexus energy control.
Multiversal Control: Affect multiple realities with GM approval.

Plasma Blade: Superheated cutting weapon.
Photon Saber: Light-based melee weapon.

War Hammer: Heavy armor-breaking melee weapon.
Battle Axe: Heavy cutting weapon.

Photon Lance: Energy spear weapon.
Energy Staff: Staff that channels energy attacks.
Rune Staff: Staff for magic or rune effects.
Twin Daggers: Fast close-range weapons.
Void Daggers: Dark-energy blades.
Hunter Bow: Silent ranged weapon.
Photon Bow: Fires light-energy arrows.
Combat Rifle: Standard ranged weapon.
Pulse Rifle: Fires energy pulses.
Gravity Rifle: Pushes, pulls, or pins targets.
Plasma Cannon: Heavy plasma weapon.
Void Cannon: Fires unstable void energy.
Energy Shield: Creates a defensive barrier.
Rune Shield: Protects against magic effects.
Power Armor: Boosts strength and defense.
Titan Armor: Heavy armor with major durability.
Adaptive Armor: Adjusts to resist damage types.
Celestial Armor: Cosmic-level armor.
Mystic Orb: Focuses magical energy.
Power Crystal: Stores energy.
Soul Crystal: Protects or stores spiritual energy.
Reality Core: Stabilizes or alters reality effects.
Void Core: Stores void energy.
Rune Stone: Activates a magic effect.
Cosmic Prism: Redirects or amplifies cosmic energy.
Celestial Relic: Powerful cosmic artifact.
Titan Relic: Boosts strength or durability.
Storm Relic: Controls wind or lightning.
World Relic: Rare artifact with GM-created world-level effects.
