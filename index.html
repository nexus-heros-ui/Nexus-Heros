Nexus Heroes Rulebook

Create heroes, build teams, complete missions, fight villains, earn rewards, and grow stronger through the campaign.

How Fighting Works

Combat is turn-based. Each character gets one turn per round. On your turn, choose Attack, Defend, Use Power, Use Equipment, Help Ally, or Move.

Combat Rolls

Use Strength for melee, Speed for dodging, Combat for fighting skill, Power Control for powers, and Intelligence for tactics or tech. The GM decides difficulty.

Damage

Compare the attacker’s stat against the defender’s Durability, Speed, or defense. The GM decides light, serious, critical, or battle-ending damage.

Power Rarity

Common 50%, Uncommon 30%, Rare 14%, Epic 4.5%, Legendary 1.3%, Nexus 0.2%.

GM Final Rule

The GM has final say on unclear rules, power limits, mission results, rewards, and campaign consequences.
