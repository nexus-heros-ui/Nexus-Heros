📖 Nexus Heroes Game Manual

⚡ Quick Start Guide

Goal:
Create heroes, join a world, travel across the map, complete missions, fight villains, earn rewards, and grow stronger.
First Time Setup:

1. GM opens the website.
2. GM clicks World Map.
3. GM creates a World Code.
4. Players create heroes in Player Portal.
5. Players join the world using the code.
6. GM adds heroes to the map.
7. GM starts Turn 1.

🦸 Player Handbook

Hero Creation Order:

1. Type a hero name or press Random Name.
2. Spin Species.
3. Spin Origin.
4. Spin Alignment.
5. Spin Power Chance.
6. If powers are granted, spin Power Count.
7. Spin Power until all slots are filled.
8. Spin all 7 stats.
9. Spin Equipment Chance.
10. Spin Equipment Count and Equipment if granted.
11. Spin Weakness Count and Weaknesses.
12. Press Generate Hero.
13. Press Save Hero.
Stats:

Strength = melee power and lifting.
Speed = movement and dodging.
Durability = defense and damage resistance.
Intelligence = tactics, tech, and problem solving.
Combat = fighting skill.
Stamina = endurance and long battles.
Power Control = accuracy and control with powers.

🎲 Game Master Handbook

GM Job:
The GM controls the world, villains, missions, rewards, consequences, events, NPCs, and unclear rules.
How To Start A Game:

1. Click World Map.
2. Type a world name.
3. Press Create World Code.
4. Give the code to players.
5. Decide Team Mode or Solo Mode.
6. Add heroes to the map.
7. Generate a mission.
8. Begin Turn 1.
Turn Order:

1. World Events happen.
2. GM moves NPCs and villains.
3. Player 1 takes a turn.
4. Player 2 takes a turn.
5. Continue until all players act.
6. Enemies act.
7. GM presses Next Turn.

⚔ Combat Guide

Combat is turn-based.

On your turn you may do one main action:

Attack
Defend
Use Power
Use Equipment
Help Ally
Interact
Move
How Attacks Work:

1. Choose an attack or power.
2. Pick the best stat for it.
3. Compare it against the enemy defense.
4. GM decides if it hits.
5. Apply damage or effects.
Defeat:
Defeated does not always mean dead. A character may be knocked out, captured, injured, forced to retreat, or removed from the fight.

🌍 World & Travel Guide

Adding Heroes To The Map:

1. Generate a hero.
2. Go to World Map.
3. Click a location.
4. Press Add Current Hero To Map.
Travel:

1. Click a location dot.
2. Press Travel Here.
3. Your hero moves to that location.
4. A travel event may happen.
Travel Events:

Safe Travel
Random Event
Villain Encounter
Merchant Found
Hidden Treasure
World Boss Appears

📜 Mission Guide

How To Generate A Mission:

1. Click a map location.
2. Press Generate Mission Here.
3. Read the mission objective.
4. Travel to the location.
5. Complete the mission.
6. Earn the reward or suffer consequences.

❓ Help / FAQ

How do I add players?
Players create heroes, join the world code, then the GM adds their hero to the map.
How do turns work?
Each player acts once. Then enemies act. Then GM presses Next Turn.
How do I move?
Click a map location, then press Travel Here.
How do I save?
Press Save Hero after generating your hero.
What does the GM do?
The GM controls missions, enemies, rewards, consequences, world events, and final rulings.
