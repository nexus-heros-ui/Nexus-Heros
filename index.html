📖 Nexus Heroes Game Manual

Quick Start

1. Create a hero in Player Portal.
2. GM creates a world code.
3. Add hero to world, map, and player manager.
4. Generate enemies and a mission.
5. Refresh fighters and start combat.

Player Handbook

Players choose actions like Attack, Defend, Heal, Heavy Attack, or Power Attack. The GM approves the action before it happens.

GM Handbook

The GM controls world events, enemies, missions, health, rewards, shops, and final rulings.

Combat

Attack = normal damage.
Heavy Attack = stronger damage but less accurate.
Power Attack = costs energy but hits harder.
Defend = adds shield.
Heal = restores health.

Inventory

Loot items can be equipped as weapons, armor, or artifacts. Gear can improve future combat systems.

Shop

Use Nexus City Shop to buy generated items with credits.

Campaign Save

Campaign Save stores players, enemies, inventory, credits, world turn, active missions, and map characters.
